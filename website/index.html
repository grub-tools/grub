How it works

Three steps. No sign-up required.

1

Scan any barcode

Point your camera at any barcode. Instant nutritional data from a database with millions of products.

2

See exactly where your calories went

Breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks—broken down by calories, protein, carbs, and fat. No guesswork.

P C F
3

Open the app and go

No account. No onboarding. No internet required. Everything works offline from a local database you own. Your food history is always available, always private.

One tool, everywhere

Phone, terminal, or self-hosted API. Same data, same simplicity.

iOS & Android

CLI

Free, open source, and fast. Track from the terminal. Pipe it, script it, alias it.

Self-hosted API

Run grub serve on your network. Access your food log from any device, no cloud required.

terminal 
# Log a meal
$ grub log "chicken breast" 200g --meal lunch

# Scan a barcode
$ grub barcode 3017620422003 --meal snack

# View today's summary
$ grub summary

  Breakfast  380 kcal   P: 28g  C: 42g  F: 10g
  Lunch      520 kcal   P: 45g  C: 38g  F: 18g
  Snack      210 kcal   P:  4g  C: 32g  F:  8g

Your data stays yours

Privacy isn't a feature. It's the foundation.

Local SQLite database. All data lives on your device in a standard SQLite file you can inspect, export, or delete at any time.

Zero telemetry. No analytics, no usage tracking. We don't know you exist and that's by design.

Open data. Built on a free, community-maintained food database. No proprietary lock-in.

Sync on your terms. iCloud, local network, or file export. You choose how your data moves between devices—if it moves at all.
