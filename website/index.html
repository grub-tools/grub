How it works
01

Scan a barcode

Point your camera at any food item. grub searches a community-maintained database of over 3 million products instantly.

02

See the full picture

Calories, protein, carbs, fat — broken down clearly. No gamification, no guilt. Just the information you asked for.

03

Log and move on

Add to your daily log with a tap. Track by meal — breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks. Open the app, close it, live your life.

Privacy

Your food log is nobody's business.

Most nutrition apps monetize your data. grub stores everything in a local SQLite file on your device. No accounts. No cloud sync you didn't ask for. No analytics. Zero telemetry.
